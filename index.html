About VARIT 2K24

Learn More about our VARIT



  • The name VARIT means variables of IT related events combined together. We came up with this name from 2011 of our IT department students. Our first VARIT was conducted on 2011 and continued yearly once by final year students and supported by both 1st year and 2nd year students.How many of us know ? Our college doesn’t get any registration fee from the participants and we provide food and hospitality with free of cost. VARIT is a legacy of our Jamal Mohamed College and IT department

  • VARIT 2K24 is an Inter-collegiate Technical Symposium for Under Graduate students of Computer Science, Computer Applications, Information Technology and Computer Science & Engg. This Intellectual fete aims at providing a platform to expose the talents of students and their Technical skills. A Technical Symposium of this kind will go a long way in bringing out the inherent talents and also sharpening the skills of the students. This will also provide a platform for Under Graduate students from different institutions offering similar kind of programmes in this part of TamilNadu to exhibit their performance. Skill Development is the need of the hour and this technical symposium is a right step in that direction.

    TECHNICAL AND NON - TECHNICAL

    TECHNICAL :



    BUGS BOSS (DEBUGGING) :

    ⦁ Only one participant per department/college will be permitted.
    ⦁ Preliminary questions are based on C,C++,JAVA & PYTHON.
    ⦁ Finals will be based on JAVA LANGUAGE.
    ⦁ Decision of Jury is final.
    ⦁ BONAFIDE CERTIFICATE MUST.






    WEB WITS (WEB DESIGN) :

    ⦁ ONLY ONE PARTICIPANT PER DEPARTMENT/COLLEGE WILL BE PERMITTED.
    ⦁ PARTICIPANT WILL BE ASKED TO DESIGN A WEBPAGE UNDER THE GIVEN TOPIC.
    ⦁ DOMAIN : HTML,CSS.
    ⦁ DECISION OF THE JURY IS FINAL.
    ⦁ BONOFIDE CERTIFICATE MUST.





    VISUAL VOYAGE (PPT):

    ⦁ TWO MEMBERS PER DEPARTMENT/COLLEGE WILL BE PERMITTED.
    ⦁ PRESENTING PAPERS MUST BE BASED ON THE GIVEN TOPICS.
    ⦁ TIME DURATION: 7 MINUTES FOR PRESENTATION AND 3 MINTUES FOR QUERIES.
    ⦁ PAPER SHOULD BE IN IEEE FORMAT AND NOT EXCESS 6 PAGES. ⦁ BONAFIDE CERTIFICATE MUST.

    TOPICS:
